About the App

The Vilanova Explorer App brings the rich history of Vilanova i la Geltrú to life. Through AR, guided tours, and interactive maps, users can immerse themselves in the atmosphere of the 18th and 19th centuries. Whether you're a local, a tourist, or a history enthusiast, this app offers an engaging way to explore the past.
